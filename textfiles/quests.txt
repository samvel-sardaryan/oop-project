Quest 1:
Title: The Haunted Ruins
Description: Rumors speak of a haunted ruins deep in the mountains. Explore the ruins, uncover their secrets, and put any restless spirits to rest.

Quest 2:
Title: The Enchanted Forest
Description: The forest has become enchanted, causing strange phenomena and endangering travelers. Discover the source of the enchantment and restore the forest to its natural state.

Quest 3:
Title: The Beast of the Swamp
Description: A terrifying creature has been sighted in the swamp, terrorizing local villages. Track down the beast, confront it, and protect the villagers from its rampage.
