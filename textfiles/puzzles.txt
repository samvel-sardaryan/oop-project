Puzzle 1:
Description: Solve the riddle; I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with wind. What am I?
Answer: echo

Description: Arrange the following letters to form a word; P N E I S A T
Answer: painters

Puzzle 3:
Description: What is always in front of you but can't be seen?
Answer: future

Puzzle 4:
Description: I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I?
Answer: pencil

Puzzle 5:
Description: I have keys but open no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter, but can't go outside. What am I?
Answer: keyboard
